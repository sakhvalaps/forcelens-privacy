The short version

Everything you need to know in 30 seconds

All log analysis runs locally in your browser. Nothing is sent to ForceLens servers.

Your Salesforce session token is used only to call Salesforce APIs on your behalf — never stored or sent to us.

AI features are fully opt-in. Your API key goes directly from your browser to your chosen provider — ForceLens never sees it.

No analytics, telemetry, tracking pixels, or advertising SDKs. Ever.

No personal data, browsing history, or log content is ever sold or shared with third parties.

Support portal submissions are optional and deletable by you at any time.

1

About ForceLens

Who we are and how to reach us

ForceLens is a browser extension that provides analysis and visualization tools for Salesforce developers. All processing occurs locally on your device within your browser.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices regarding that information. If you have questions, please contact us using the details at the bottom of this page.

3

Browser permissions

Why each permission is required

Storage
Required to retain your debug logs, preferences, and settings on your local device between sessions. All stored data remains on your device and is never transmitted to ForceLens.
Cookies
Required to read your active Salesforce authentication token, which is needed to make authenticated requests to Salesforce on your behalf. This token is used only for that purpose, is held in memory for the duration of the request, and is never stored or transmitted to ForceLens.
Tabs
Required to open and manage the ForceLens workspace tab. No information about your other open tabs, their content, or their URLs is accessed or transmitted.
Active Tab
Required to identify the Salesforce page you are currently viewing when you activate the extension, so the correct context can be loaded in the workspace.
Salesforce Domains
Required to provide functionality within Salesforce. Covers all supported Salesforce deployment environments. No page content from these domains is transmitted to ForceLens.
External AI Services
Required only if you choose to configure an AI provider. Requests are made directly from your browser to your chosen provider using credentials you supply. ForceLens does not intermediate or have visibility into these requests.
Support Services
Required only for the optional Support portal. Used solely when you voluntarily submit feedback, bug reports, or feature requests. No Salesforce data or log content is ever transmitted through this channel.
4

AI features

Optional and fully under your control

AI-powered features within ForceLens are disabled by default and require you to actively configure them using credentials from an AI provider of your choosing.

When you use AI features, a structured, non-sensitive summary of your log data is transmitted to your chosen provider. Raw log content is not transmitted. Your API credentials are stored only on your device and are sent directly to your provider — ForceLens does not have access to them at any point.

The privacy practices of your chosen AI provider are governed by that provider's own privacy policy. We encourage you to review it before enabling AI features.

5

Data storage & retention

Where your data is stored and for how long

Locally stored data — including log content, preferences, and any AI credentials you configure — is stored on your device and persists until you remove it, clear your extension data, or uninstall ForceLens. This data is never accessible to ForceLens.

Support portal submissions are stored on secure, access-controlled infrastructure. You may request deletion of your submissions at any time through the Support portal or by contacting us directly.

Authentication tokens used to interact with Salesforce are held in memory only for the duration of the relevant request and are discarded immediately afterwards.

6

Third-party services

How we work with external services

Support infrastructure — We use third-party cloud infrastructure to operate the optional Support portal. Only content you voluntarily submit is stored. No Salesforce data, log content, or credentials are ever transmitted through this service.

AI providers — When you enable AI features, your browser communicates directly with your chosen AI provider. ForceLens does not act as an intermediary and has no access to these communications. The provider's own privacy policy applies to any data you transmit to them.

ForceLens does not use advertising networks, analytics platforms, tracking technologies, or any other third-party data collection services.

7

Children's privacy

Our policy regarding minors

ForceLens is a professional tool intended for use by adults. We do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under the age of 13. If you have reason to believe that a minor has submitted personal information through our Support portal, please contact us and we will take prompt action to remove it.

8

Your rights & choices

Controls available to you at any time

Remove local data

You may clear all locally stored data at any time through your browser's extension settings, or by uninstalling ForceLens.

Delete support submissions

Any content you have submitted through the Support portal may be deleted at any time directly from within the portal.

Opt out of AI features

You may disable AI features and stop all related data transmission at any time by removing your AI credentials from the extension settings.

Contact us

You may contact us at any time with questions or requests regarding your data. We will respond promptly.

9

Changes to this policy

How we communicate updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal, operational, or regulatory reasons. When we make material changes, we will update the effective date at the top of this page and notify users through the extension. Your continued use of ForceLens following any such update constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
